About

Mental health awarness specially among teenagers

Giving exposure to things more than academics to ameliorate the overall health of students including mental health and developing possibilities for underprivileged students across the country.

01
LIVE SESSIONS

  • Live sessions will be conducted on a weekly basis by experts from various fields like newspaper editors, psychology & stress management experts, career guidance and other extra and co-curricular domains.
  • Thus we decided to bring the platform online
  • students can ask their queries

02
Enhancing knowledge and experience of teenagers

  • Content Creation : We have created a diverse collection of audios and animated videos on hobbies and various interest areas of students of different age groups to enhance mental health under the guidance of psychologists and make them available on public platforms like Youtube for easy & free access.
  • Competitions : Quizzes & other types of competitions based on conducted live sessions will be held regularly.
  • Practical Experiences : We planned to provide practical experiences for 10th grade students in the form of various internships.
