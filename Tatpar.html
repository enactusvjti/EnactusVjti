Impact

Idiological Impact

01
Empowerment

  • We are looking forward to empower at least all 5000 dabbawalas by providing them extra income.

  • virtue of this project , we are heading to help them empower and stand up keeping the buisness idea intact and undisturbed.

02
Employment

  • Beside these 5000 Dabbawalas, we are going to employ new faces from rural parts in this chain.

  • This will increase manpower and efficiency of the project providing work to their hands at the same time .

03
Cost Reduction

  • In Mumbai today, cost of express delivery is high. We are heading to reduce this cost, therefore helping common people .

  • This will, in turn , increase the productivity , standards and competition in the express delivery market in Mumbai

04
Environment Friendly

  • We're going to use the traditional Dabbawala transport and chain system using bicycles and Mumbai's local electric trains.

  • This will cost nothing to environment but will cut a part of pollution that could have been, if someone else is delivering.
