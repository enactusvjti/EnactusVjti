About Enactus(Global)

Enactus (formerly known as Students in Free Enterprise) is an international, non-profit organization of students, business and academic leaders, committed to improve the quality of life and standard of living of those in need by the power of entrepreneurial action. Enactus is spread across 39 countries, over 1600 universities and has over 67,000 students as active members.

En actus students take up community outreach projects to transform lives of people in need and shape a better world.

  • Entrepreneurial : Having visionary leadership & perspective to see an opportunity.
  • Action : The enthusiasm and willingness to do something and the commitment to shape a better world.
  • Us : A group of people connected together to turn this action into reality.

About Enactus VJTI

Enactus VJTI was started in August 2019 which works for the sustainable development of the underprivileged people. We a team of 23 students have put together our efforts in the spirit of Enactus. To bring about serious changes in the society, we the students of Enactus VJTI try to facilitate our capabilities and knowledge to the people in need but also guide and inspire the deprived through our dexterity and expertise that we develop over time. We are effectively using the entrepreneurial action to empower people, to improve their livelihoods, and to bring quantifiable change in the society.

Projects

With the support of Enactus India and business advisory from SPIT, we have taken 3 projects to date.

PROJECT TATPAR

Mumbai Dabbawalas: Globally famous & appreciated community for their outstanding service with a unique business model and service system. Over 5000 Dabbawalas are serving around 2 Lakh people every day. Project “Tatpar” is all about uplifting this “White Hat Army”, to make them as prosperous as their legacy, using their own identity skill of goods’ delivery, and to generate admirable revenue.

Read More

PROJECT NIRMAL

The pandemic created a great chaos; people are maintaining social distance in-order to fight this. Taking into consideration of the current situation, Project “Nirmal” brings a solution to tackle this situation. Project “Nirmal” includes, foot operated mechanism by which doors of toilet can be operated by the help of our feet. This will tend to maintain the handless contact that is the need of time in this very current situation. This project will bring a change in the way the world tends to move in a contactless experience.

PROJECT EKLAVYA

According to recent research, teenager's stress levels during the school year have far exceeded what they believe to be healthy, many teens also have reported feeling overwhelmed and depressed or sad as a result of stress.With the help of our project "Eklavya" we aim at giving exposure to things more than academics to ameliorate the overall health of students including mental health & developing possibilities for unprivileged students across the country.
